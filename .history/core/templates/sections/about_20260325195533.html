About Me

From the shores of Dar es Salaam to building digital products loved worldwide

Hello! I’m Allan Deus, a passionate full-stack developer and creative technologist based in the beautiful coastal city of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

My journey began with a simple curiosity about how websites worked — and it quickly turned into a lifelong obsession with building digital experiences that feel alive.

Over the past 2 years, I’ve had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and visionary entrepreneurs across Africa and beyond. From developing high-performance e-commerce platforms to creating AI-powered dashboards, I thrive on turning complex challenges into simple, beautiful, and scalable solutions.

What sets me apart is my blend of technical excellence and design sensitivity. I don’t just write clean code — I craft interfaces that users remember and products that make people say “Wow, this feels different.”
